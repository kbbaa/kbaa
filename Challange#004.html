Neon Pulse GEOINT Writeup

ID: #NP-101 Date: 2025-12-29 Auth: KBAA

Objective Summary

A rogue offshore facility was transmitting signals from an unmapped location. The extraction required identifying the exact coordinates using satellite imagery and analysis of environmental lighting conditions visible in leaked CCTV frames.

Step 01: Light Emission Analysis

Analyzed the flicker frequency of neon signs in a leaked video. The frequency was specific to a power grid standard only used in a few territories, significantly narrowing the search area.

Step 02: Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) Analysis

Used SAR imagery to penetrate persistent cloud cover over the target zone. Identified the unique structural footprint of the facility's specialized cooling towers.

Extraction Result: SUCCESS

"Facility located 120km off the coast of the target territory. Operation Neon Pulse has provided the first clear map of the facility's external infrastructure. Intelligence forwarded."
